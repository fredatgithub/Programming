You can see teh Khnude was a puppy then. We put him next to the other puppy since we had two at the time. He was the bigger one with brown eyes in the litter, and was extremely friendly to people. Summer 2004

This is just one year later and look how big he has gotten. Khnude now weighs 75 punds and stands three feet tall at the shoulder. We hired a trainer who came to the house to train him. Khnude did really well with that and knows all of his commands, such as sit, stay, and lay down.

We also had an invisable fince installed around the front yard. This is so helpful, especially since we live on a relitively busy street. It makes us feel more comfortable that he is safe, and the small children who live nearby do not have to worry about a large dog approaching them.
