Facts about horses

  • Horses are domesticated mammals that are used by humans as a means of transportation or work.
  • Generally speaking, horses have four distinct gaits, or speeds, that they move at.
  • Domestic horses have a lifespan of around 25 years.
  • A 19th century horse named ‘Old Billy’ is said to have lived 62 years.
  • If you are planning on showing or riding horses, you must know that there are two main types of saddles to choose from: Western and English.
  • Scientists believe that horses have evolved over the past 50 million years from much smaller creatures.
  • You should groom your horse or the horse you are working with before and after you ride, making sure you clean out their hooves.
