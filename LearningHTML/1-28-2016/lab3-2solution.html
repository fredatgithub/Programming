After School Program Volunteers Needed!

Central High School is seeking volunteers to work with children in an after school program. Volunteers will work with groups of children in reading circles, fun and game activities homework helpers, and listening time. Children of all ages are bused to the high school to participate in the activities. All volunteers can get credit for time spent helping the young students.

Bring your after school fun ideas and suggestions. Snacks are served and bus pick-up begins at 5:00 p.m. Please join us for some fun and donate your time to do this extraodinary program.
