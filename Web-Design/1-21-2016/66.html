Phi Kappa Phi

Main Topics

Phi Kappa Phi is an academic honor society for superiour students in all fields. Phi Kappa Phi became national in 1900 and has conintued to grow continuously since then. Local chapters distribute approcimately $150,000 annually in additional awards and scholarships as well as support a variety of campus programs that carry out the Societys aim of reconizing and promoting academic excellence
